Convert the technical indicators into actual backtestable strategies by adding explicit BUY and SELL rules for each indicator.

Use these rules:

1. RSI Strategy
- Calculate RSI(14).
- BUY when RSI crosses above 30 after being below 30.
- SELL when RSI crosses above 70 or crosses below 50 after entry.

2. ADX-DI Strategy
- Calculate ADX(14), +DI, and -DI.
- BUY when +DI crosses above -DI and ADX > 25.
- SELL when -DI crosses above +DI or ADX falls below 20.

3. ATR Breakout Strategy
- Calculate ATR(14).
- BUY when Close breaks above the previous 20-day high plus 0.5 × ATR.
- SELL using a trailing stop: Close < highest close since entry - 2 × ATR.

4. SMA Crossover Strategy
- Calculate SMA(50) and SMA(200).
- BUY when SMA(50) crosses above SMA(200).
- SELL when SMA(50) crosses below SMA(200).

5. EMA Crossover Strategy
- Calculate EMA(12) and EMA(26).
- BUY when EMA(12) crosses above EMA(26).
- SELL when EMA(12) crosses below EMA(26).

6. MACD Strategy
- Calculate MACD using 12, 26, 9 settings.
- BUY when MACD line crosses above the signal line.
- SELL when MACD line crosses below the signal line.

7. Bollinger Band Mean Reversion Strategy
- Calculate Bollinger Bands using SMA(20) and 2 standard deviations.
- BUY when Close crosses back above the lower band after closing below it.
- SELL when Close reaches the middle band or upper band.

8. Stochastic Strategy
- Calculate %K and %D using standard 14, 3 settings.
- BUY when %K crosses above %D while both are below 20.
- SELL when %K crosses below %D while both are above 80.

9. CCI Strategy
- Calculate CCI(20).
- BUY when CCI crosses above -100.
- SELL when CCI crosses below +100 or below 0.

10. MFI Strategy
- Calculate MFI(14).
- BUY when MFI crosses above 20.
- SELL when MFI crosses below 80 after previously being above 80.

11. OBV Strategy
- Calculate OBV and OBV SMA(20).
- BUY when OBV crosses above OBV SMA(20).
- SELL when OBV crosses below OBV SMA(20).

12. VWAP Strategy
- Calculate VWAP.
- BUY when Close crosses above VWAP and Volume is above Volume SMA(20).
- SELL when Close crosses below VWAP.

13. Supertrend Strategy
- Calculate Supertrend using ATR period 10 and multiplier 3.
- BUY when Supertrend turns bullish.
- SELL when Supertrend turns bearish.

14. Parabolic SAR Strategy
- Calculate Parabolic SAR.
- BUY when Close crosses above SAR.
- SELL when Close crosses below SAR.

15. Williams %R Strategy
- Calculate Williams %R(14).
- BUY when Williams %R crosses above -80.
- SELL when Williams %R crosses below -20.

16. ROC Momentum Strategy
- Calculate ROC(12).
- BUY when ROC crosses above 0.
- SELL when ROC crosses below 0.

17. Donchian Channel Strategy
- Calculate 20-day high and 10-day low.
- BUY when Close breaks above the previous 20-day high.
- SELL when Close breaks below the previous 10-day low.

18. Keltner Channel Strategy
- Calculate Keltner Channel using EMA(20) and ATR(10).
- BUY when Close breaks above the upper Keltner Channel.
- SELL when Close falls below the middle Keltner line.

For all strategies:
- Use long-only trading.
- One position at a time.
- Generate signals after market close.
- Execute trades at the next trading day open.
- Include transaction fees.
- Avoid look-ahead bias.
- Save all completed trades into trade_history.csv.
- Each trade row must include region, ticker, company_name, strategy, buy_date, buy_price, sell_date, sell_price, holding_period, roi_pct, trade_return, and exit_reason.
- If a strategy has an active open BUY position at the end of the test period, record it in latest_signals.csv but do not force it into trade_history.csv unless the framework supports unrealized trades separately.
