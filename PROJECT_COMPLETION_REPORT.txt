IMPORTANT NOTES
================================================================================

1. DATA DOWNLOAD:
   The system uses Yahoo Finance via yfinance library.
   It will download real data when you run `python main.py`.
   Note: International tickers may have connectivity issues.
   
2. NETWORK/SSL:
   Some tickers may fail if there are SSL certificate issues.
   These are logged and don't stop the process.
   Success/failure logged in results/combined/ CSVs.

3. ASSUMPTIONS:
   - Next-day execution (signals at close, trade at open)
   - No look-ahead bias in signal generation
   - 0.1% commission only (real costs may be higher)
   - No slippage or market impact
   - No stop-losses or take-profits
   - Long-only strategy

4. LIMITATIONS:
   - Past performance ≠ future results
   - Historical backtests don't guarantee live performance
   - Parameter sensitivity (small changes = big impact)
   - Data quality depends on Yahoo Finance
   - Some newer IPOs may have insufficient history
